# User Guide

## Roles
InkLink supports three primary user roles:

1.  **User**: Clients looking to browse tattoos and book appointments.
2.  **Artist**: Tattoo artists showcasing their portfolio and managing bookings.
3.  **Studio Owner**: Managers of physical studio locations, overseeing multiple artists.

## Features by Role

### For Users
*   **Explore**: Browse the "Feed" to find tattoos by style or location.
*   **Booking**: Use the "Book Now" button on an artist's profile to start a project.
    *   **Dossier Wizard**: A step-by-step process to describe your idea, upload references, and set a budget.
*   **Dashboard**: Track the status of your booking requests in `/dashboard`.

### For Artists
*   **Profile**: Your public dossier (`/artist/username`). Showcase your bio, styles, and portfolio.
*   **Portfolio Manager**: Upload high-quality images of your work. Tag them with styles for better discovery.
*   **Booking Management**: Receive booking requests (Dossiers). Accept, reject, or request more info.
*   **Calendar**: Set your availability for appointments.

### For Studios
*   **Studio Page**: A public landing page (`/studio/studio-name`) listing all resident artists.
*   **Member Management**: Invite artists to join your studio.
*   **Analytics**: (Coming Soon) View booking trends and revenue.
